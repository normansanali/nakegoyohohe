Who Is BdoubleO100's Wife, Nicole? Here's A Look At Their Relationship

BdoubleO100 is an American Youtuber known for making content about the sandbox video game Minecraft.

He is one of the most popular Minecraft players on the website. He also goes by the following names:

  • BdoubleO
  • Bdouble0
  • Bdubs

His YouTube name refers to the first three letters of his last name: Booko. 

The YouTuber is also a member of the Hermitcraft Minecraft server and is one of the Hermits. Before being a member of the Hermitcraft server, he was a member of Mindcrack, a Let'sPlay collective. 

BdoubleO100 is one of the longest-lasting Minecraft channels on all of Youtube and has been active since 2011. He has released more than 3000 videos on the topic of Minecraft and has become something of an elder statesman when it comes to the YouTube Minecraft community. 

He is also a family man and loves his family dearly. Some of his most heartwarming content is when he is playing Minecraft with his daughter.

Who Is BdoubleO100's Wife?

The Minecraft Youtuber BdoubleO100 is married to his wife Nicole Booko and has been so for a long time.

Nicole is 40 years old, though her date of birth is unknown. She is a social media influencer and has 13.7K followers on her Instagram page. Besides being BdoubleO100's wife, Nicole is also known for being a model on the LTK website.

How Many Children Do BdoubleO100 And Nicole Have?

The Minecraft YouTuber BdoubleO100 and his wife, Nicole Booko, have two children.

Their two children, daughters, are named Ariana and Eden. Ariana was born on July 16, 2014, and Eden was born on May 21, 2016. BdoubleO100, Nicole, and their two children featured quite prominently on BdoubleO100's on one of the Youtuber's side channels called BdoubleOLive.

The channel, which he started on January 24, 2012, has charted the growth of both of the couple's daughters. The first video on the channel was posted on February 4, 2012, and initially began as a podcast.

This podcast, called Chit Chat's with Josh & John, lasted for two episodes, then went defunct. Two years after the end of the podcast, the channel became active again, but this time as a family vlog.

BdoubleO100 started posting short videos of his newborn daughter, Ariana. The videos started charting the little girl's growth.

Soon, the videos started showcasing the Youtuber's personal life alongside his daughter's growth. The family would post such videos as Ariana's first steps, and her first words, alongside videos of the family traveling to various places like the zoo.  The channel also became a place for BdoubleO100 and Nicole to talk about their relationship, and the two talked about how they met and their marriage.

Soon, with the birth of Eden, the channel became even more of a family vlog. 2016, Eden's birth year, was perhaps the busiest year for the channel. The channel went into a hiatus following the birth and unfortunate passing away of their third daughter Ivy Rose. The channel returned for a while three years ago, but after posting nine videos, it went into hiatus again and hasn't been updated.

What Happened To BdoubleO100's Third Daughter?

Sadly, the Minecraft YouTuber BdoubleO100 and Nicole Booko's third daughter could not make it to life.

Ivy Rose was born on January 12, 2018, but passed away eighteen hours later due to a genetic condition called Trisomy 13.

Trisomy 13, or Patau Syndrome, is a chromosomal abnormality where some or all of the body's cells contain extra genetic material from chromosome 13, thus the name. 

The extra genetic material from chromosome 13 disrupts normal development and causes multiple complex organ defects that eventually cause loss of life.

A Look At BDoubleO100's YouTube Career: Mindcrack

One of the reasons why BdoubleO100 grew rapidly on Youtube during his early days was by joining Mindcrack.

Mindcrack is a group of prominent content creators that publish footage of their video gaming experience online. It started as a Minecraft-oriented server but has since become something much larger. 

BdoubleO100 joined the server back in September 2011. He was invited to join the server by fellow Youtuber Guude, who now goes by GuudeBoulderfist.

BdoubleO100 was a member of the Mindcrack server for four years until the server dismissed him on April 3, 2015. 

According to a statement released by the Youtuber, he parted ways with the server due to legal changes within the brand that made it so that he was not allowed to be an official member anymore. His dismissal caused some uproar against the server, which has since lessened.

How Does BdoubleO100 Make Money?

Like most other Youtubers, BdoubleO100 makes money through his channel's views, sponsorship deals, and Patreon account.

YouTube pays its UK, US, and Canadian creators $2 - $12 per 1000 monetized views after taking its cut. This money comes from AdSense, the Ads that Youtube runs on its videos.

The longer a viewer stays on a video, the more money the creator makes. Supposing that BdoubleO100's videos retain a large portion of the total views he has received on his main channel, which is 446,968,145, he has made roughly $5,363,617 from his main channel.

Using the same metric on his other channels, the Youtuber has made $245,343 from the total views BdoubleOLive has amassed and $8,754 from the 729,566 total views BDUBSwithCheese has amassed.

There's also his Patreon page. Patreon is a website where people can support their favorite content creators by sending them monthly donations.

Most people with a Patron account have tiers where people are given certain benefits depending on which tier they are a part of. BdoubleO100 is no difference.

However, what makes him different is that while most accounts have three tiers, he only has two. They are:

  • Tier 1: $5 per Month: Bdubs Resource Pack Downloads
    • The member gets access to the Bdubs Minecraft Resource Pack
  • Tier 2: $20 per month: Bdubs Servers
    • The member gets access to a Patreon-only Minecraft server
    • They will be provided with a private community chat where BdoubleO100 will be active
    • They will get all the perks of the lower tier
